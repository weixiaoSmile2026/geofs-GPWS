# GEOfs-GPWS Digital Instruments


✈️ GeoFS Ultimate HUD & GPWS Pro (v10.3 Official)
Description:
This professional-grade add-on provides a high-precision digital overlay for GeoFS, displaying Airspeed, Altitude, and Vertical Speed with zero lag. It features advanced flap detection and engine monitoring, alongside a high-fidelity GPWS (Ground Proximity Warning System).


Key Features:


Smart V-Speed Engine: Automatically calculates V1 and VR speeds based on your current flap configuration—a feature rarely seen in other scripts.


Visual & Audio GPWS: Every warning is displayed as a high-visibility text alert on the HUD, ensuring safety even if you are flying without headphones/speakers.


Comprehensive Warnings: Includes Terrain/Sink Rate, Gear/Flaps reminders, Stall, and Overspeed alerts.


Adaptive Overspeed Logic: Unlike basic scripts, this one calculates overspeed limits based on altitude! It enforces a 250kt limit below 10,000ft and dynamically adjusts for thinner air as you climb higher.


Ground-Zero AoA Fix: Exclusively corrects the common GeoFS bug where Angle of Attack (AoA) displays incorrectly on the ground, ensuring your HUD reads 0.0° until the moment of rotation.
